Master's Thesis: Position Control and Locking of Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor for Propeller Drives in Drones

Motor Control ChibiOS RTOS Drone Motor

Implements a state machine for speed and position control of an electric motor. It focuses on integrating position control with minimal hardware implementation. The state machine is built into the open-source RTOS (ChibiOS), that the open-source motor controller firmware (VESC) is built with.

Conference Paper IECON 2022: Position Locking for Permanent Magnet Synchronous Machine Propeller Drives in Drones by Hall-Effect Sensor-Assisted Nonlinear Observer

Motor Control ChibiOS RTOS Drone Motor

The paper presents a hall-effect sensor-assisted non-linear observer-based solution for position locking of a surface-mounted permanent magnet synchronous motor (SMPMSM) propeller drive in drone applications. The paper is based on the Master's Thesis.
